Upper Whittleton Arch

Authored by: Haley Johnson

Whittleton arch is a beautiful landform that is more than just an arch. This landform has beautiful waterfalls and an amazing scenic hiking trail. All around the area, the river/creek area is influenced by the limestone in the area, giving it a unique appearance and even a disappearing stream.

There are also two arches near this site. upper and lower Whittleton Arch. These both are relatively the same in appearance, just at different elevations. I found out that lower Whittleton arch has the largest mass of any arch at Red River Gorge.
